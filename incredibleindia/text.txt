One of the oldest civilisations in the world, India is a mosaic of multicultural experiences. With a rich heritage and myriad attractions, the country 
is among the most popular tourist destinations in the world. It covers an area of 32, 87,263 sq. km, extending from the snow-covered Himalayan heights 
to the tropical rain forests of the south. As the 7th largest country in the world, India stands apart from the rest of Asia, marked off as it is by 
mountains and the sea, which give the country a distinct geographical entity.



Food & Cuisine:- "The flavours of India"

Known for its diversity that varies with every region, state, community, culture and even religion, food in India is a vibrant assortment of 
uncountable dishes. It is distinctly characterised by the subtle and sophisticated use of spices, grains, fruits and vegetables that are all 
grown in the country.
